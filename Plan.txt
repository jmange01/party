Goal:
- A web app that allows people who love games to connect with others like them. 
  Focus on meeting new people and encouraging in-person connection.

User Stories:

- Opening an account

- Creating a profile (questionnaire?)
    Sign up with email & PW -> select UserName -> Enter location -> Select interests

- Logging into account
    authenticate -> Verify sign up complete -> Drop into sign up flow if necessary -> Land on main page (parties?)

- Updating profile
    add interests, change location, change UserName(?)

- Finding a group

- Starting a group

- Joining a group

- Inviting new members

- Chatting with a group

- View joined groups

- Leaving a group
